- Startbootstrap-personal
- Constra-bootstrap-vsc
- Star-admin2
- Light-bootstrap-dashboard
- Bootstrap-dashboard-2.1.1-vsc
- Material-dashboard
- CoolAdmin-master

These 10 themes passed the test.
